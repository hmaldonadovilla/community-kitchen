This procedure must be read by all employees of the Community Kitchen.
It must be made available to volunteers involved in cutting vegetables, meal preparation and/or receiving food deliveries for guidance.

## RESPONSIBILITY

Cooks are responsible. Volunteers may help under supervision.
Kitchen Manager provides daily oversight. Operations Manager holds overall accountability.

>## IMPORTANT
>
>1. General rules:
>
>- All storage check activities must be performed at the beginning of AM and PM shifts and recorded in the Storage & Cleaning Checks app.
>- Always apply First In, First Out (FIFO) for storage and utilization. Older products to be stored in front or at the top for easy access to be used first.
>- Immediately inform Kitchen Manager or Operations Manager when stock is running low.
>- Any issue → record in the app + inform Kitchen Manager or Operations Manager immediately.
>
>2. Hygiene:
>
>- Discard immediately: expired, undated, moldy, spoiled, pest-damaged, leaking or swollen items.
>- Label with sticker: leftovers, cut vegetables, opened dairy, opened jars/cans/bottles → product name + production date or opening date.
>- Keep shelves, seals, containers and crates clean and dry.
>- Check for pests (insects, droppings, gnaw marks). Any sign → record in app + inform Kitchen Manager or Operations Manager.
>
>3. Temperature:
>
>- Fridges: 0–7 °C
>- Freezers: ≤ –12 °C
>- If out of range: keep door closed, move food if needed, discard if unsure → record in app, inform Kitchen Manager or Operations Manager.

## Fridges

### ALLOWED

- Dairy products
- Leftovers (sealed & dated), max 48 hours
- Cut vegetables (sealed & dated), max 48 hours
- Opened jars/bottles
**Eggs in original carton and stored in staff fridge only**

### NOT ALLOWED

- Raw meat or raw fish
- Hot or warm food
- Unsealed and/or undated items

## Freezers

### ALLOWED

- Purchased frozen vegetables
- Purchased frozen animal protein
- Food cooked on site and frozen by staff
- Cut vegetables frozen by staff

### NOT ALLOWED

- Hot or warm food
- Partially thawed food
- Damaged or leaking packaging
- Unsealed and/or undated items

## Fresh Vegetables areas

### ALLOWED

- Uncut in clean crates or containers or packaging
- Off the floor
- Kept dry

### NOT ALLOWED

- Directly on the floor
- Under sinks or near cleaning products
- Moldy, stale or spoiled vegetables

## Dry good areas

### ALLOWED

- Unopened dry goods, e.g. rice, pasta, lentils
- Unopened cans and jars
- Unopened spices and condiments

### NOT ALLOWED

- Open bags without closing / sealing
- Wet / damp packaging
- Directly on the floor
- Under sinks or near cleaning products
